France Travel Guide

Things to Do


Eiffel Tower:

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France.The tower is 324 metres (1,063 ft) tall, about the same height as an 81-storey building, and the tallest structure in Paris. Its base is square, measuring 125 metres (410 ft) on each side.

🎫Ticket: 16,70€/Adult

⏰Suggest Stay Time: 2-3 Hours



The Hunchback of Notre-Dame:

Notre-Dame, is a medieval Catholic cathedral on the Île de la Cité in the 4th arrondissement of Paris. The cathedral was consecrated to the Virgin Mary and considered to be one of the finest examples of French Gothic architecture. The cathedral's construction began in 1163 under Bishop Maurice de Sully and was largely complete by 1260.

🎫Ticket: €8.50/Adult

⏰Suggest Stay Time: 5-6 Hours
